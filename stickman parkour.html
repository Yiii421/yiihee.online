How to Play

Control your stickman character using keyboard arrows or WASD keys. Jump over obstacles, run up walls, and slide under barriers to reach the finish line. Timing is everything in this fast-paced parkour adventure.

The game rewards precision and speed, so practice your moves to achieve the best completion times. Each level introduces new challenges that will test different aspects of your parkour skills.
